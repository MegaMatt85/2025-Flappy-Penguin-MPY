**2025 CSE Design Competition, Team Flappy Penguins**

•	Given two weeks to design and embed a stuffed toy with hardware or software functionality within a team of four. \n
•	Although not flawlessly executed, we created an ATMega329P Xplained Mini controlled penguin alarm clock which would wait for the inputted time, chirp and flap its wings using servos when it goes off, snooze when yelled at, and turn off when pressing its nose. \n
•	Played a key part in writing the firmware to control the timer, implementing a power-saving mode to reduce energy consumption overnight, and processing the sequence of events when the alarm goes off. \n
